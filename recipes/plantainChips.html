Crispy plantain chips

Plantain chips are a great alternative to potato chips.

Ingredients:

  • 1 green plantain
  • 1/2 tbsp avocado oil
  • sea salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. With a small knife, make 3-4 cuts lengthwise down the plantain, just enough to go through the peel. Then remove the peel.
  3. Thinly slice the plantain with a mandoline (on the thinnest setting). Add the plantain slices to a bowl and toss with the avocado oil to evenly coat.
  4. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and place the plantain slices on the tray in one layer. Sprinkle with sea salt and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until just golden along the edges.
